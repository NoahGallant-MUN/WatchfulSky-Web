Watchful Sky
Google Play GitHub
WatchfulSky allows you to track satellites in real time and view the current arrangement of the planets.

See the current locations of your favourite satellites or find out where they will be later. Satellites can be added to your favourites list for quick access. Watch satellites as they fly over your head.

Ever wonder where they other planets are? WatchfulSky will show you each planet in our solar system in scrollable solar system.

The WatchfulSky logo was designed and realized by Matt Kennedy
